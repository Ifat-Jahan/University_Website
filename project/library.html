Library

The central library building is within the walking distance from the academic buildings and students residences. It is a compact building with limited built-in facilities to provide teaching aides such as reading facility, borrowing of books, journals etc. to the students and teachers of RUET. In consistent with the academic curricula, the contents of the central library are being updated consistently to keep up with modern technological trends.
